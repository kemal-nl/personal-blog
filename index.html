Welcome

Welcome to my personal learning blog. This blog contains my notes for the fast.ai Practical Deep Learning for Coders course as well as for the fast.ai Live Coding Sessions. This site is built with [fastpages](https://github.com/fastai/fastpages), an easy to use blogging platform with extra features for Jupyter Notebooks.

My Learning Approach

For each lesson of Practical Deep Learning for Coders I am taking the following steps: 1. Watch the week's lecture passively 1. Watch it again and take notes 1. Based on lecture do a mini-project 1. Read corresponding chapter(s) of the fast.ai book, take notes, do quizzes Besides Practical Deep Learning for Coders, I am also following the live coding sessions. Notes can be found [here](#live-coding). My goal for now is to be able to compete and win on Kaggle. Besides that I want to get in the habit of producing rather than consuming content. Each day I will do some work (however little it may be). My hope is that, over time, it will compound and I can get very good at data science and programming.
